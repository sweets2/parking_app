# F-31 — Fix Tow Segment Projection Bugs

## Summary

Fixes two systemic bugs in tow zone segment polyline rendering:

1. **Wrong-location lines** — Signs from out-of-borough addresses (e.g. "1523-1535 ADAMS ST" at lat 40.7558, north of the previous Overpass bbox) had their segments projected onto the nearest Hoboken road segment hundreds of metres away, producing a line at the wrong location with no tow marker nearby.

2. **Horizontal heuristic fallback for northern streets** — Streets at Hoboken's northern boundary (12th–14th St) were absent from `road-geometry.json` because the Overpass bbox (`40.734–40.750`) cut them off. Segments fell back to a perfectly horizontal E-W line that didn't match the road.

## Root causes

- `getSubsegment` had no maximum snap distance. Any sign was projected onto the nearest segment regardless of how far away that segment was.
- The Overpass bounding box in `build-road-geometry.ts` ended at lat 40.750, clipping 12th–14th St and northern address ranges.

## Changes

### `app/map.ts`

**`getSubsegment`** — Add `MAX_SNAP_M = 50`. After finding `bestDist`, return `[]` if `bestDist > MAX_SNAP_M`.

**`renderTowSegments`** — Replace the `if/else` OSM-vs-heuristic branch with an `if` + `if (waypoints.length < 2)` cascade so an empty `getSubsegment` result falls through to the orientation-correct heuristic at the sign's actual GPS location.

### `fetcher/build-road-geometry.ts`

Expand Overpass bbox from `(40.734,-74.041,40.750,-74.025)` to `(40.733,-74.042,40.756,-74.024)` to capture 12th–14th St and northern address ranges.

### `data/road-geometry.json`

Regenerated with the expanded bbox: 82 streets / 495 ways (up from 60 streets / ~350 ways). Now includes 12TH ST, 13TH ST, 14TH ST, and Adams St coverage through lat 40.756.

## Tests added (`tests/unit/map.test.ts`)

- `getSubsegment`: sign > 50 m from all ways → returns `[]`
- `renderTowSegments`: geometry exists but sign is > 50 m away → heuristic fallback draws 2-point segment at sign's actual lat

## Given/When/Then

- GIVEN a sign whose GPS lat is > 50 m from all segments of its named street in road-geometry.json, WHEN renderTowSegments is called, THEN the segment is drawn at the sign's actual GPS location (not at the distant road segment)
- GIVEN a sign on "14TH ST", WHEN renderTowSegments is called after the geometry rebuild, THEN the segment follows the OSM road centerline (not a perfectly horizontal fallback)
