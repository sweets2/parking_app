# CF-26 — Side-Specific Sinatra Drive North Source Correction

## Module

`data/street-cleaning.json`, `data/street-cleaning-overrides.json`,
`data/cross-streets.json`, `fetcher/geocode-cross-streets.ts`,
`shared/cleaning-geometry.ts`, `tests/unit/fetch-street-cleaning.test.ts`,
and `tests/unit/map.test.ts`.

## Problem

The checked-in street-cleaning source data has some numbered-street sides ending at Hudson St.
even though observed ground rules continue to Sinatra Drive North:

- Eleventh St. north: `Hudson St. to Willow Ave.`
- Eleventh St. south: `Willow Ave. to Hudson St.`
- Twelfth St. north/south: `Willow Ave. to Hudson St.`
- Fourteenth St. south: `Willow Ave. to Hudson St.`

For Fourteenth St., the correction is side-specific: the south side continues east past Hudson
St. only about halfway to Sinatra Drive North for the Monday 8-9 am rule. It does not go all
the way to Sinatra Drive North. The north side is correctly bounded by Hudson St. for the
Wednesday 8-9 am rule and must not extend toward Bernard McFeeley Shipyard Lane.

There is also a normalization gap. `normalizeToGeometryKey("Sinatra Drive North")` currently
returns `SINATRA DR NORTH`, while the road geometry key is `SINATRA DR N`. Even if the source
location text is corrected, runtime clipping cannot use the `SINATRA DR N` geometry unless the
normalizer maps that display label to the existing geometry key.

## Goal

Correct the affected side-specific source data and make future scrapes preserve the
corrections. Ensure runtime street-cleaning highlighting can clip Sinatra Drive North,
midway-to-Sinatra, and Hudson St. endpoints correctly.

## Changes

### `data/street-cleaning-overrides.json`

Add two source overrides:

- `Eleventh St.` / `North` / `Hudson St. to Willow Ave.` -> `Sinatra Drive North to Willow Ave.`
- `Eleventh St.` / `South` / `Willow Ave. to Hudson St.` -> `Willow Ave. to Sinatra Drive North`
- `Twelfth St.` / `North` / `Willow Ave. to Hudson St.` -> `Willow Ave. to Sinatra Drive North`
- `Twelfth St.` / `South` / `Willow Ave. to Hudson St.` -> `Willow Ave. to Sinatra Drive North`
- `Fourteenth St.` / `South` / `Willow Ave. to Hudson St.` -> `Willow Ave. to Midway to Sinatra Drive North`

The overrides are matched by the existing CF-24 override mechanism.

### `data/street-cleaning.json`

Update the currently checked-in generated data to match the override results, so the app is
correct before the next network scrape.

### `shared/cleaning-geometry.ts`

Extend `normalizeToGeometryKey` so `Sinatra Drive North` maps to the existing road-geometry key
`SINATRA DR N`, and `Midway to Sinatra Drive North` maps to the synthetic endpoint key
`MIDWAY TO SINATRA DR N`.

### `app/map.ts`

Add targeted endpoint coordinate overrides for Fourteenth St.:

- `14TH ST|HUDSON ST` clips at the Hudson St. centerline and does not leak east toward
  Bernard McFeeley Shipyard Lane or Sinatra Drive North.
- `14TH ST|MIDWAY TO SINATRA DR N` clips halfway between Hudson St. and Sinatra Drive North.
- `14TH ST|SINATRA DR N` remains available for explicit checks and future data, but is not used
  by the Fourteenth St. north row.

### `fetcher/geocode-cross-streets.ts` and `data/cross-streets.json`

Add a static coordinate for `sinatra drive north` and the synthesized cache entries
`11th street|sinatra drive north`, `12th street|sinatra drive north`, and
`14th street|sinatra drive north`, plus `midway to sinatra drive north` and
`14th street|midway to sinatra drive north`. This prevents click-time segment detection from
relying on live Nominatim for the corrected east endpoints.

## Acceptance criteria

Given the checked-in `data/street-cleaning.json`,
When Eleventh St., Twelfth St., and Fourteenth St. entries are inspected,
Then Eleventh St. north includes `Sinatra Drive North to Willow Ave.`
And Eleventh St. south includes `Willow Ave. to Sinatra Drive North`.
And Twelfth St. north and south include `Willow Ave. to Sinatra Drive North`.
And Fourteenth St. south includes `Willow Ave. to Midway to Sinatra Drive North`.
And Fourteenth St. north remains `Hudson St. to Willow Ave.`.

Given an Eleventh St. north source row with `location: "Hudson St. to Willow Ave."`,
When `applyStreetCleaningOverrides` is called with the Eleventh St. override,
Then the returned location is `Sinatra Drive North to Willow Ave.`
And one applied override log line is returned.

Given a Twelfth St. source row with `location: "Willow Ave. to Hudson St."`,
When `applyStreetCleaningOverrides` is called with the Twelfth St. override,
Then the returned location is `Willow Ave. to Sinatra Drive North`
And one applied override log line is returned.

Given a Fourteenth St. south source row with `location: "Willow Ave. to Hudson St."`,
When `applyStreetCleaningOverrides` is called with the Fourteenth St. override,
Then the returned location is `Willow Ave. to Midway to Sinatra Drive North`
And one applied override log line is returned.

Given a Fourteenth St. north source row with `location: "Hudson St. to Willow Ave."`,
When `applyStreetCleaningOverrides` is called with only the Fourteenth St. south override,
Then the north row remains `Hudson St. to Willow Ave.`
And no applied override log line is returned.

Given road geometry keyed `11TH ST` or `12TH ST`, plus `SINATRA DR N`, and `WILLOW AVE`,
When `renderViolationHighlights` renders an active corrected numbered-street entry ending at
`Sinatra Drive North`,
Then a polyline is rendered,
And the rendered longitude range reaches both the Sinatra Drive North end and the Willow Ave. end.

Given road geometry keyed `14TH ST`, `WILLOW AVE`, and the Fourteenth St. midpoint override,
When `renderViolationHighlights` renders the active Fourteenth St. south entry with
`location: "Willow Ave. to Midway to Sinatra Drive North"`,
Then the rendered longitude range reaches east past Hudson St.
And it stops before Sinatra Drive North.

Given road geometry keyed `14TH ST`, `HUDSON ST`, and `WILLOW AVE`,
When `renderViolationHighlights` renders the active Fourteenth St. north entry with
`location: "Hudson St. to Willow Ave."`,
Then the rendered longitude range stops at Hudson St.
And it does not extend east to Sinatra Drive North or Bernard McFeeley Shipyard Lane.

Given `data/cross-streets.json`,
When the app seeds the geocode cache,
Then `11th street|sinatra drive north`, `12th street|sinatra drive north`, and
`14th street|sinatra drive north`, plus `14th street|midway to sinatra drive north`, are
available without a live geocode call.
