# CF-18 — Fix Madison / Monroe Cross-Street Contamination (getCrossStreetCoord Range Filter)

## Module
`app/map.ts` — `getCrossStreetCoord`, `parseLocationBounds`

## Problem
For N-S streets like MADISON ST and MONROE ST, `getCrossStreetCoord` selects the
cross-street point whose longitude is closest to the main street's median longitude.
Contaminated JC-Heights geometry for 15TH ST and 11TH ST includes points south of
Hoboken (~40.732, ~40.729) whose longitudes happen to be near Madison/Monroe's
longitude. Those contaminated points win the nearest-longitude race, returning a wrong
latitude. `parseLocationBounds` then clips to a tiny range near Newark instead of the
full block span, so the segment never renders green or red at the correct location.

- Madison: `15TH ST → lat 40.732` (wrong; real ~40.756). Clip collapses to [40.732, 40.737].
- Monroe:  `11TH ST → lat 40.729` (wrong; real ~40.750). Clip collapses to [40.729, 40.737].

This is distinct from CF-17 (2ND ST): orientation is correctly classified as N-S; only
the wrong candidate point within a valid cross-street key is being selected.

## Fix
Add `validRange?: [number, number]` to `getCrossStreetCoord`. When provided, skip any
candidate point whose returned coordinate (lat for N-S lookup, lng for E-W lookup) falls
outside the range. In `parseLocationBounds`, compute `latRange`/`lngRange` from the main
street's own geometry extent (padded by `RANGE_PAD = 0.003°`) and pass it to both
cross-street lookups in both the N-S and E-W branches.

`RANGE_PAD = 0.003` is a **contamination filter**, not a precision tolerance. It is
intentionally 10× larger than the visual `PAD = 0.0003`. Genuine cross-street
intersections lie within the main street's span; out-of-borough contamination sits
0.005–0.01° outside it.

## Behavioral contract
- GIVEN a N-S main street whose cross-street geometry contains contaminated points
  outside the main street's latitude span,
  WHEN `getCrossStreetCoord` is called with `validRange`,
  THEN contaminated points are skipped and the real in-range point is returned.

- GIVEN Madison St. (N-S) with a contaminated 15TH ST point at lat 40.732 and a real
  point at lat 40.756,
  WHEN `renderViolationHighlights` is called at an active time,
  THEN rendered polylines have all lats > 40.734 and at least one lat > 40.750.

- GIVEN Monroe St. (N-S) with a contaminated 11TH ST point at lat 40.729 and a real
  point at lat 40.750,
  WHEN `renderViolationHighlights` is called at an active time,
  THEN rendered polylines have all lats > 40.734.

- GIVEN the CF-17 2ND ST scenario (fallthrough triggered by MARSHALL ST distance > 0.005°),
  THEN the range filter does not affect the fallthrough — MARSHALL ST still fails the
  distance check regardless of latRange, so fallthrough still triggers.

## Test list
- GIVEN Madison St. (N-S) with contaminated 15TH ST geometry south of Newark,
  WHEN renderViolationHighlights at active time,
  THEN all rendered lats > 40.734 and max lat > 40.750
- GIVEN Monroe St. (N-S) with contaminated 11TH ST geometry south of Newark,
  WHEN renderViolationHighlights at active time,
  THEN all rendered lats > 40.734
