## Hard constraints

- No JavaScript in `index.html` beyond the two `<script>` tags (Leaflet CDN and `app.js` module).
- `#map` uses `height: 100vh` — do NOT use `calc(100vh - 180px)` or any other height.
- `#sign-list` is `display: none` — do not unhide it.
- `#here-btn` and `#clear-btn` are `display: none` initially — controlled by `app/ui.ts`.
- `#query-bar` and `#query-active-bar` must both be present with the exact IDs shown.
- Leaflet script must be loaded before `app.js`.
- `app.js` script must use `type="module"`.
